Sirens

A Siren, according to Greek mythology, is a creature who is half bird and half woman who lures sailors to their doom with their enchanting singing voice. Sirens are commonly conflated with mermaids in popular media and culture, who have been given traits belonging to Sirens. The mermaids in the Pirates of the Carribean films are a relevant example.
